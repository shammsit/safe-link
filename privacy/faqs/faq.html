Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

SAFE LINK – Community Based Emergency Response & Public Safety System

What is Safe Link? ▼
Safe Link is a community-based emergency response system that allows users to send instant SOS alerts with live location to nearby users, volunteers, and authorities for faster assistance.
Who can use Safe Link? ▼
Anyone can use Safe Link, including students, women, elderly people, travelers, and individuals who want quick help during emergencies.
What problems does Safe Link solve? ▼
Safe Link reduces emergency response time by instantly notifying nearby people and authorities, ensuring quicker help during critical situations.
What happens when I press the SOS button? ▼
When you press the SOS button, your live GPS location is captured and an emergency alert is sent instantly.
What personal information does Safe Link collect? ▼
We collect name, phone number, email ID, and profile details.
Is my location tracked all the time? ▼
No, location is only accessed during emergencies or when permission is granted.
Does Safe Link sell my data? ▼
No, Safe Link does not sell or share user data.
Is my data secure? ▼
Yes, data is secured using Supabase, HTTPS, and authentication systems.
Does Safe Link work on all devices? ▼
Safe Link works on most smartphones and modern browsers.
Does it require internet? ▼
Yes, internet is required for real-time alerts.
What happens if GPS is off? ▼
Location accuracy may reduce and delay help.
